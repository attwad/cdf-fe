Transcriptions cost 0.006$ per 15 seconds, that's 1.20$ an hour, the average lesson time.

What happens when you donate?

When the donation is received, new lessons are automatically and randonmly scheduled to be transcribed depending on how long their are and the amount you donated.
Every cents of the donations will be used and will be used for transcriptions only.
When the transcriptions are scheduled, this page will display which lessons were scheduled thanks to you, exciting!

Why can't I chose the lessons I want transcribed?

Consider this an experiment, I believe that the value of this website and indexing of the audio transcripts comes from the serendipity in search results, surprising people by matching their search queries with lessons they wouldn't have suspected existed.
